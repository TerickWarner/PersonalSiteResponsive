About

Professionally...

I am a self taught developer currently focusing on leveling up my hard and soft skills. I am a strong believer in self motivation and self-learning and I strive to bring that attitude in my everyday life. I am not afraid to ask questions, collaborate, and I take constructive criticism well.

In my free time...

You will see me walking my dog, Zippy, at the library reading the latest fantasy or horror novel, or hanging out with my family and friends. I am always up for new adventures and would love to travel more in the coming years.
